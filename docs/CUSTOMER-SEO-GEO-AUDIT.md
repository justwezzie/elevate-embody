# Free SEO and GEO Audit

Prepared for: Elevate + Embody  
Prepared by: Creatovation Studio  
Date: April 10, 2026

## Overview

We reviewed the Elevate + Embody website from the perspective of:

- SEO: how well the site is positioned to appear in traditional search results
- GEO: how well the site is positioned to be understood, quoted, and surfaced by AI-powered search and answer engines

This audit focuses on visibility, clarity, trust, and conversion potential.

## Headline Result

The website has a strong brand feel and a clear service offering, but there are important opportunities to improve how well it communicates with search engines and AI-powered discovery tools.

At the moment, the site is doing a good job visually, but there is still room to make it work harder as a lead-generation and booking tool.

## What the Website Is Already Doing Well

- The brand identity is clear and memorable
- The visual design feels calm, premium, and consistent
- The booking journey is straightforward
- The services are understandable once users spend time on the site
- The site now includes stronger FAQ and informational content

These are strong foundations. The next step is making sure search engines and AI tools understand the business as clearly as a human visitor does.

## Key Opportunities

### 1. Clearer visibility in search

The site can be strengthened so search engines more confidently understand:

- who the business is
- where it is based
- what services are offered
- who teaches the sessions
- how people can book

Why this matters:

If those signals are not as strong as they could be, the site may miss opportunities to appear for local and service-based searches, even when the offer is a good fit.

### 2. Better AI-search readiness

More people are now finding businesses through AI summaries, answer engines, and conversational search tools rather than only typing short queries into Google.

The site benefits from having:

- stronger business facts
- clearer question-and-answer content
- better structured information about sessions and services

Why this matters:

If the site does not present information in a way AI systems can easily interpret, it becomes less likely to be surfaced, summarized, or recommended when people ask for nearby or relevant wellness, yoga, or boxing options.

### 3. Stronger conversion from information pages

The site already has a good tone and a clear booking path, but there is still an opportunity to guide users more confidently from:

- curiosity
- to trust
- to booking

Why this matters:

Many potential customers need reassurance before booking. They want quick answers about pricing, suitability, location, what to expect, and who they are booking with. The clearer and more strategically presented those answers are, the more likely the site is to convert.

### 4. More authority and trust signals

The site presents the business well, but it can go further in building trust around:

- expertise
- local relevance
- service clarity
- consistency across key pages

Why this matters:

When a site reads as trustworthy and well-structured, it doesn’t just help rankings. It also helps people feel safer booking.

## What This Means for the Business

Right now, the website looks good and explains the brand well, but there is an opportunity to turn it into a stronger growth asset.

With the right SEO and GEO improvements, the website can do more of the following:

- attract more qualified organic traffic
- appear more often in local and service-led searches
- become easier for AI engines to reference
- answer objections before they become drop-offs
- increase direct bookings through the website

## Overall Assessment

From a customer-facing business point of view, our view is:

- Visual brand presence: strong
- Search visibility potential: good, with clear room to grow
- AI discoverability potential: promising, but worth improving deliberately
- Conversion support: solid foundation, but not yet maximised

## Recommended Next Step

The website would benefit from a focused SEO and GEO optimisation phase designed to:

- strengthen how the business is understood online
- improve visibility in search and AI discovery
- make service information easier to trust and act on
- support more direct bookings

This is not about changing the whole brand or rebuilding the site. It is about making the current site work harder and more intelligently.
